Idli

Description

Idli is the main breakfast recipe in South India mainly in states of Kerala,Tamilnadu, Karnataka, and AP

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Black lentils
  • yeast
  • salt
  • water

Steps

  1. Soak both rice and black lentils in water for atleast 7 hrs
  2. Grind or use a mixer to grind both ingredients and mix them
  3. Let the batter ferment overnight , you can add yeast to speed up the process
  4. Finally steam bake them in morning
