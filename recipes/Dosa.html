Dosa

Description

Dosa is the main breakfast recipe in South India mainly in states of Kerala,Tamilnadu, Karnataka, and AP

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • fenugreek seeds
  • urad dal
  • salt
  • water

Steps

  1. Soak all the ingredients in water for atleast 7 hrs
  2. Grind or use a mixer to grind both ingredients and mix them
  3. Let the batter ferment overnight , you can add yeast to speed up the process
  4. Finally the batter is spread in a circular motion until it fills the pan, add some oil, then flipped to other side and cooked for a minute
